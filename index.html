Welcome

This is my very first webpage!

In this site, I will summarize some interesting data points from a study of top 100 billboard titles from the 1950's to 2017.

Through referencing the Spotipy Web API wrapper, Brandon Ferro, Christopher Newey and I were able to find and compare how song characteristics have changed over time relative to the 1950's and the beginnings of the top 100 billboard charts. This insight into popular music is also a reflection upon our culture. Enjoy your visit!
